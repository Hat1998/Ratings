Ibis Hotel Riyadh

Located 5 minutes’ walk from the tallest building in Saudi Arabia, the Kingdom Tower, Ibis Riyadh Olaya Street offers free Wi-Fi and a 24-hour front desk. Al Faisaliyah Centre is 5 minutes away by car. All accommodation at Ibis Riyadh Olaya Street have a modern décor with wooden floors. Each room comes with a minibar, an electric kettle and a private bathroom. Dining options at Ibis Riyadh Olaya Street include an all-you-can eat breakfast buffet. With a selection of pasta or grilled meat, Oopen Pasta & Grill gives you the chance to make your own recipe. Café 24/7 serves pastries, snacks and beverages. You can shop and enjoy a day out at Olaya Mall, just a 5-minute drive away from Ibis Riyadh Olaya Street. The property features meeting rooms and free luggage storage. Dry cleaning services can be arranged for a fee. Riyadh International Airport is 35 km from Ibis Riyadh Olaya Street. An airport shuttle is available upon request, and free valet parking is provided. Ibis Riyadh Olaya Street has been welcoming Booking.com guests since 16 Dec 2012. Hotel chain/brand: ibis
