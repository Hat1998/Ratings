Ramada by Wyndham Hotel Riyadh

Ramada by Wyndham Hotel Riyadh is situated near King Abdul Aziz Road in central Riyadh. This 4-star hotel offers deluxe accommodation with 24-hour front desk, LED Smart TVs, free internet access and parking. All of the guest rooms are spacious and decorated in a classical style. Each room includes a seating area, satellite channels and internet connection. There is an on-site café with pool views and the hotel’s restaurant is also open for lunch and dinner, serving international cuisine. Leisure facilities at Ramada by Wyndham Hotel Riyadh include an outdoor swimming pool and a modern fitness centre. King Abdullah Park is 2.3 km from the hotel, while King Abdulaziz Historical Center is 2.8 km away. The nearest airport is King Khalid, 32 km from Ramada by Wyndham Hotel Riyadh, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service. Ramada by Wyndham Hotel Riyadh has been welcoming Booking.com guests since 14 Jan 2010. Hotel chain/brand: Ramada
