Braira Hotel

Set in Riyadh, 10 km from Al Nakheel Mall, Braira Qurtubah provides concierge services and free WiFi throughout the property. Offering a restaurant, the property also has a fitness centre, as well as an indoor pool and a sauna. The accommodation offers room service, a business centre and luggage storage for guests. The hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms offering a desk, a coffee machine, a minibar, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a shower. All rooms include a kettle, while selected rooms also offer a kitchenette with a fridge, a microwave and a stovetop. At Braira Qurtubah all rooms are equipped with bed linen and towels. A buffet, continental or American breakfast can be enjoyed at the property. The accommodation offers a sun terrace. Speaking Arabic, German, English and Hindi at the 24-hour front desk, staff are always on hand to help. Saqr Aljazeera Aviation Museum is 11 km from Braira Qurtubah, while Riyadh Park is 16 km from the property. The nearest airport is King Khalid International Airport, 17 km from the hotel. Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 8.1 for a two-person trip.
